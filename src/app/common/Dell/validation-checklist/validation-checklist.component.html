Visual Mechanical Inspection (VMI)

  • Gaps/ broken and loose hinges
  • ports - damage and corrosion
  • cosmetic damage
  • battery latching
  • dirt
  • screen latching
  • missing screws
  • gently shake to check for loose objects
  • feet and LCD bumpers
  • if unit has screw-less case, perform VMI of insides
  • Clean Unit with Tech Spray
